About This Project

CyberSec is a free and open-source platform designed to bring together the most valuable cybersecurity resources in one place. From tools and learning labs to bug bounty writeups, courses, and reference materials, everything is organized to help learners, security researchers, and professionals find what they need quickly and efficiently.

The platform is continuously updated with new resources and community contributions to ensure access to relevant and high-quality content. Built and curated by x606, this project is continually evolving. Feel free to explore the sections above, or contribute on GitHub to help expand the arsenal!
